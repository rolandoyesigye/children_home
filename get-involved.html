In-Kind Donations

Beyond financial support, donating essential items directly impacts the daily lives of our children. We are grateful for every contribution.

Food & Nutrition

  • Rice, beans, maize flour
  • Cooking oil & spices
  • Milk powder & baby formula

Clothing & Bedding

  • New or gently used clothes
  • Bed sheets & warm blankets
  • School uniforms & shoes

Education Supplies

  • Notebooks, pens & pencils
  • Storybooks & textbooks
  • Backpacks & art materials

Health & Hygiene

  • Soap, toothpaste & detergent
  • First aid kits & basic medicine
  • Sanitary products

Play & Growth

  • Toys, puzzles & board games
  • Sports balls & skipping ropes
  • Musical instruments

Digital Literacy

  • Used laptops or tablets
  • Educational software
  • Solar lamps for studying

Want to organize a drop-off?

Please contact us before sending items to ensure we can receive them and that they meet the current needs of the home.
