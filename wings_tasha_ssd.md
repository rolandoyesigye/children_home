# Site Structure Document (SSD)
## Wings of Tasha Children's Home
**Tagline:** Every Child Deserves A Second Chance
**Location:** Rubaare, Ntungamo District, Uganda
**Founded:** 10th September 2025

---

## 1. Overview

This document defines the complete page structure, navigation hierarchy, and content sections for the Wings of Tasha Children's Home website. It is intended to guide designers, developers, and content creators during the build process.

---

## 2. Site Goals

- Introduce the organisation and establish credibility with donors, partners, and the community.
- Clearly communicate the mission, vision, and programs of the home.
- Provide a frictionless path for visitors to donate, volunteer, or partner.
- Share stories and updates that build long-term supporter relationships.
- Serve as a reference point for governance, policies, and transparency.

---

## 3. Target Audience

| Audience | Primary Need |
|---|---|
| Individual donors (local & international) | Learn about the cause and give easily |
| Volunteers | Understand roles and apply |
| NGOs / Faith-based orgs / Corporates | Partnership and grant information |
| Community members | Refer children in need |
| Government / Regulatory bodies | Governance and legal compliance |
| Media / Press | Background information and press materials |

## 10. Notes for Developers

- **CMS recommended:** WordPress, Webflow, or similar — to allow the team to update news and stories without developer help.
- **Mobile-first:** Many visitors in Uganda will access via mobile. Ensure fast load times and mobile-optimised layouts.
- **Language:** English (primary). Consider adding Runyankore/Rukiga in future phases for local community outreach.
- **Accessibility:** Use alt text on all images, especially photos of children.
- **Child safety:** Do not display full names or identifying information for children in the home without explicit consent documentation.
- **Donation integration:** Consider Africa-specific payment options (MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money) alongside international options (PayPal, bank transfer).
